About the Event

Women in Tech Awareness Week is a student-led initiative focused on promoting inclusivity, innovation, and equal opportunities for women in the technology sector.

Through talks, discussions, and networking, the event creates a supportive space for students to learn, connect, and confidently explore careers in technology.
